Call for Abstracts

The organizers invite the abstracts of the research works from the participants for oral and poster presentations. The abstracts must be original and unpublished. Abstracts (not more than 250 words) must include Manuscript title, Author(s), Affiliation(s)/contact details, with a clear indication of research work, methodology, major results and conclusions in the following format: Font size -12 points; Type -Times New Roman; Spacing -1.5. Kindly submit your paper in MS Word through email to icammm@adhi.edu.in. The deadline for submission of abstracts is April 29, 2024. After the presentation, authors are expected to submit the full length papers as per the format informed later.
