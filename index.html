International Conference on communication network and AI in computing (iccnaic-2024)
may 2-3, 2024
about

The International Conference on Communication Networks and AI in Computing (ICCNAIC-2024) is scheduled to be held at the Department of Electronic and Communication Engineering, Adhi College of Engineering & Technology, from May 2-3, 2024.

The primary goal of ICCNAIC-2024 is to serve as a leading international platform for the exchange of knowledge and outcomes in the areas of communication networks and AI computing theory, methodology, and applications. This conference aims to create a worldwide stage for experts, professors, scholars, and industry professionals to convene, share cutting-edge advancements, and promote collaborative efforts. Moreover, the event is designed to facilitate the transformation of fundamental research into established and industrial practices, accelerating the implementation of innovative solutions in real-world scenarios. Scholars and researchers worldwide are cordially invited to participate in the conference, share valuable insights and best practices, and explore opportunities for collaboration and advancement in the field.
